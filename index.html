Why Are Egg Prices Rising?

A closer look at what's behind your costly omelets

📈 Price Trends

Egg prices have increased by over 70% since last year due to a combination of supply chain disruptions and avian flu outbreaks.

🛒 What's Causing It?

  • Widespread avian flu affecting flocks
  • Rising feed and fuel costs
  • Supply chain delays

🥚 What Can You Do?

Consider local farmers markets, reduce waste, or try plant-based alternatives like flax eggs or aquafaba.
